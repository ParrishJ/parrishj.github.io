My Work

While working in the industry, I have collaborated with designers and developers to transition web designs into functioning websites and ecommerce storefronts for reputable brands such as Coconut Bliss, Everyday Bliss, National Extinguisher Service, and The Bread Stop. In addition to writing code, my role as a web developer included optimizing and preparing images for web projects using Adobe Photoshop, Bridge, and Illustrator. I have also maintained websites across various platforms including Squarespace, Americommerce, and ExpressionEngine. My work in this area involved implementing changes to websites to meet client needs, quickly and efficiently resolving bugs, and posting new blogs and announcements.

When working on personal projects, I use a number of tools and technologies to develop content for the web including HTML, CSS (LESS / SCSS), JavaScript, React, Redux, GatsbyJS, Material UI, Bootstrap, and Photoshop. I’m always working on new and interesting projects - stop by jaredparrish.com to see more of my work!
